Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We're here to help you get started.

How does the 30-day free trial work?

Your free trial gives you full access to all features of your selected plan for 30 days. No credit card is required to start. At the end of the trial, you can choose to upgrade to a paid plan or cancel with no obligation.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer multi-location support?

Yes, our Enterprise plan includes multi-location management with centralized billing, reporting across all locations, and location-specific access controls for staff members.

Is there a mobile app for members?

Yes, all plans include a branded mobile app for your members (available on iOS and Android) where they can book classes, check schedules, make payments, and track their attendance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), ACH bank transfers, and PayPal. We also support recurring billing for member convenience.
