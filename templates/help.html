Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new member?

To add a new member, navigate to the "Members" section from the sidebar and click on the "Add New Member" button. Fill in the required details and save.

How do I view the dashboard?

The dashboard is the first screen you see after logging in. You can also access it by clicking on "Dashboard" in the sidebar.

How do I edit a member's details?

Go to the "Members" list, find the member you want to edit, and click on the "Edit" icon next to their name.

How do I freeze or unfreeze a membership?

In the member's profile, you will find an option to change their membership status. You can select "Freeze" and specify a reason and duration.

How do I generate an invoice?

Invoices are automatically generated based on membership plans. You can view them in the "Billing" section.

How do I record a payment?

From the "Billing" section, you can select an unpaid invoice and click on "Add Payment" to record the transaction.

How do I add a new trainer?

Navigate to the "Trainers" section and click "Add New Trainer". Fill in their details, specialization, and schedule.

How do I view attendance reports?

Go to "Attendance" = "Attendance Report" to generate and view attendance records for members and trainers.

Can I export data?

Yes, most tables in the software have an "Export" button that allows you to download data in CSV or PDF format.

How do I change my password?

You can change your password from the "Settings" page, accessible from the sidebar.

How do I configure membership plans?

Navigate to "Management" = "Membership Plans" to add, edit, or delete membership packages.
